FunCodec

A Fundamental, Reproducible and Integrable
Open-source Toolkit for Neural Speech Codec

Features

Reproducible

FunCodec reproduces state-of-the-art models, including SoundStream, Encodec and so on.

Integrable

FunCodec can be easily extended to downstream tasks, such as ASR and TTS.

Efficient

FunCodec can train models on distributed GPUs, and inference in batch mode.

FreqCodec

FunCodec natively supports frequency domain, which is more suitable for speech signals.

Semantic Augment

FunCode models can be augmented with semantic tokens, such as phonemes and Hubert embeddings.
